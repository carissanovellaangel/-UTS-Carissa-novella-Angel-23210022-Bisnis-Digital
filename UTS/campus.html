My Campus


It is encouraged by a strong desire and desire to participate in the process of enlightenment of the nation and is a continuation of the secondary education that has been organized by the Yayasan Gereja Protestan Kampung Bali (YGPKB), which is the extension of the ministry mandated by the Gereja Kristen Kalimantan Barat (GKKB) Pontianak Church in the field of education, and with the national spirit and union of Indonesia on October 13th 2019 was formed the Kompartemen Pendidikan Tinggi (KomDikti) by YGPKB Pontianak with the aim as an organizer for the establishment of higher education named Institut Teknologi dan Bisnis Sabda Setia. (ITBSS). After a long process, then thanks to the grace and help of the Most Good Lord, the Decree of the Minister of Education, Culture, Research and Technology No. 460/E/O/2021 concerning the Permission to Establish the Institut Teknologi dan Bisnis Sabda Setia in the City of Pontianak Province of Western Kalimantan, organized by the Foundation of the Yayasan Gereja Protestan Kampung Bali, may be published and submitted directly by the Head of the XI Higher Education Services Institution, Prof. Dr. H. Udiansyah, M.S. on November 11, 2021.


The vision of ITBSS is a higher education institution based on Christian values in the preparation of superior human resources.


The mission of ITBSS is to educate and train human resources that are characterized, skilled, ready to compete globally and have a positive impact on their environment.

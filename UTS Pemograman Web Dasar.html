ITBSS's Major

The Digital Business study program prepares graduates who possess superior digipreneur skills and are capable of developing and applying intelligent digital technologies. Students will be equipped with theory-based learning, practice-based, and modern teaching facilities so that students can gain an optimal learning experience. The Digital Business study program has three major concentrations namely Digital Marketing, Technopreneur, and Digital Business Analyst.

The Entrepreneurship study program aims to prepare students to become Creativepreneurs who are able to identify and implement new ideas, concepts, and strategies in planning, managing, and developing a business or business.

The Information Systems and Technology program combines information systems and information technology to meet the challenges of technological development and rapid digital transformation. Information systems and technologies learn how to design and implement digital products (applications, websites, and design) that fit business goals and solve problems in everyday life.

My Favourite

Matcha

Matcha is a high-grade green tea ground into powdered form. The green tea powder is whisked into hot water, instead of steeped, to form a frothy drink. The meditative act of preparing, presenting, and sipping matcha is the backbone of the Japanese tea ceremony.

Chili Oil

Chili oil is a condiment made from vegetable oil that has been infused with chili peppers. Different types of oil and hot peppers are used, and other components may also be included. It is commonly used in Chinese cuisine, Southeast Asian cuisine, Italy, and elsewhere.

Avocado Juice

Avocado juice, or jus alpukat in Indonesian, is a rich and silky chilled beverage made of fresh avocado, blended with fresh milk and condensed milk. Luscious and thick, the drink is actually closer to a shake than a juice, and you will need a straw or a spoon in order to enjoy it.

Salmon Mentai

Salmon Mentai-don is a delicious, savoury, creamy rice dish topped with aburi (flame seared) salmon and a creamy mayonnaise mixed with fish roe (tobiko) or shrimp roe (ebiko). It's a cheat version of a mentaiko don made with just handful ingredients. Mentaiko is cod roe that's that's brined whole.
